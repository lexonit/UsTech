Your FAQs.

Let’s work together to build the perfect Salesforce solution for your business!

What is Salesforce consultation?

Salesforce consultation is a service offered by experts in the Salesforce platform who work with businesses to understand their needs and provide customized solutions for implementing and using the platform effectively.

What are the benefits of Salesforce consultation?

The benefits of Salesforce consultation include tailored solutions for your business, improved processes, better customer relationships, and increased efficiency, which can drive growth and increase return on investment..

Who should consider Salesforce consultation services?

Businesses of all sizes and industries that use the Salesforce platform can benefit from consultation services. This includes those looking to optimize their platform, those facing implementation challenges, or those looking to maximize their investment in Salesforce.

What is included in a Salesforce consultation?

A Salesforce consultation typically includes an assessment of your business needs, a review of your existing Salesforce implementation, and a tailored implementation plan. The consultant will also provide recommendations and best practices for using the platform effectively.

What services does Salesforce Support offer?

The support plans include various aspects of managing the Salesforce environment, including users, access, data, issue fixes, enhancements, etc.

How can I opt for Anvisol’s Salesforce Support?

Anvisol will recommend the best plan that fits your business needs based on the number of users, total requests raised daily, issues, and business process complexity. You can choose from flexible options based on duration or the number of support agents required.

What type of support is available through Salesforce Support?

Salesforce Support includes any ongoing activity for the smooth functioning of Salesforce, such as user creation, access provisioning, data loads, enhancements, etc.

What is the cost for subscribing to support?

Anvisol offers flexible options based on your support needs. Smaller businesses usually require on-demand support, medium-sized businesses need a dedicated team for certain hours, and larger firms need dedicated teams. The support is cost-effective and prioritized based on your requirements.

Can the support team make minor changes that I need?

Yes, Anvisol ensures that business operations are optimized. If any minor changes or enhancements are needed, the team will assist based on the agreed-upon scope of work.

What is Salesforce Sales Cloud?

Salesforce Sales Cloud is a cloud-based sales automation platform that provides businesses with a suite of tools to streamline their sales processes and maximize their sales potential.

What are the key features of Salesforce Sales Cloud?

Some key features of Salesforce Sales Cloud include lead and opportunity management, forecasting, account management, and reporting and analytics.

How does Salesforce Sales Cloud integrate with other Salesforce products?

Salesforce Sales Cloud integrates with other Salesforce products, including Marketing Cloud, Service Cloud, and Community Cloud, to provide a single source of truth for customer data and enable businesses to deliver personalized experiences to their customers.

Can Salesforce Sales Cloud be customized to meet the specific needs of my business?

Yes, Salesforce Sales Cloud is highly customizable and can be tailored to meet the specific needs of your business.

How does Salesforce Sales Cloud improve sales performance?

Salesforce Sales Cloud improves sales performance by streamlining sales processes, empowering sales teams with the tools they need to work more efficiently and effectively, and providing a 360-degree view of the customer, enabling sales teams to deliver personalized experiences.

What is Service Cloud and what does it do?

Service Cloud is a platform for customer service that enables businesses to offer omnichannel support and resolve customer issues faster and more efficiently.

How can Service Cloud improve my customer service operations?

Service Cloud helps improve customer service operations by providing businesses with a comprehensive platform for customer service. It streamlines processes and provides valuable insights and analytics.

Does Service Cloud provide omnichannel support?

Yes, Service Cloud provides omnichannel support, allowing customers to receive support through their preferred channel (email, phone, chat, or social media).

Can Service Cloud be integrated with other systems and tools I already use?

Yes, Service Cloud offers a variety of integrations with other systems and tools, such as CRM, analytics, and e-commerce platforms.

What is Marketing Cloud and what does it do?

Marketing Cloud is a comprehensive platform for digital marketing that provides businesses with the tools needed to engage customers across email, social media, mobile, and web.

How can Marketing Cloud help my business grow?

Marketing Cloud helps businesses grow by personalizing and automating marketing efforts, analyzing customer behavior, and optimizing campaigns for better results.

Can I integrate Marketing Cloud with other systems and tools I already use?

Yes, Marketing Cloud offers a variety of integrations with other systems, such as CRM, analytics, and e-commerce platforms.

Is Marketing Cloud suitable for businesses of all sizes?

Marketing Cloud is flexible and scalable, making it suitable for businesses of all sizes, from small start-ups to large enterprises.

What is Commerce Cloud?

Commerce Cloud is a cloud-based eCommerce platform designed to help businesses sell products and services online with a personalized, omnichannel shopping experience.

How does Commerce Cloud enhance the online shopping experience?

Commerce Cloud enhances the online shopping experience by providing a seamless and personalized customer journey across all devices and channels.

Can Commerce Cloud integrate with other Salesforce products?

Yes, Commerce Cloud can be easily integrated with other Salesforce products like Marketing Cloud, Service Cloud, and Sales Cloud to create a unified customer experience.
